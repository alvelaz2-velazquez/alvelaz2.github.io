About DeepCuts

We find the music the algorithm missed.

What is DeepCuts?

DeepCuts is a music discovery site built for people who are tired of hearing the same songs over and over. We focus on finding the hidden gems, the deep cuts, the songs that never got the attention they deserved but absolutely should have.

Unlike streaming algorithms that just show you more of what you already listen to, DeepCuts uses real human curation. You tell us a song you love, and we find something new for you based on actual taste and knowledge, not just data.

Why We Built This

♫ Real Curation

Every recommendation on this site comes from a real person who actually listened to it, not an algorithm pulling from your listening history.

🎥 Music + Visuals

We believe some songs only make complete sense when you see the music video too. We try to bring both the audio and visual experience together in one place.

🎵 All Platforms

Not everyone uses the same streaming service. We try to link to Spotify, YouTube, and Apple Music wherever possible so you can listen however you want.

How It Works

The process is pretty simple. You enter a song that you love into the search bar on the homepage. From there, one of our human reviewers looks at your pick, thinks about the vibe, the mood, the genre, and everything else that makes that song what it is, and finds you something you probably haven't heard before but will love.

We're not trying to replace Spotify or Apple Music. We just want to be the place you come to when you feel like you've heard everything and need something fresh.
